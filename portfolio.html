Battery Thermal Management System Using Cold Plates in Electric Vehicles

8th Semester | April 2025

Advanced thermal management solution for lithium-ion batteries in electric vehicles, investigating passive, active, and hybrid models based on thermal and hydraulic performance.

  • Cold Plates: Designed heat exchangers as cooling interfaces to directly absorb and transfer heat from battery modules to coolant systems
